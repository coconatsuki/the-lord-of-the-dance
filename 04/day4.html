The Desert of Rhythms

The Dune Dash

The familiar coolness of the Enchanted Forest fades behind Jimli as he marches forward. Slowly, the trees thin out, giving way to rocky outcrops and eventually, the endless sea of sand. 'So, this is it, eh? The great Desert of Rhythms,' he mutters. 'Looks like I'm about to find out what it feels like to have sand in places sand was never meant to be.'

As Jimli steps into the desert, the heat hits him like a forge blast. 'Never thought I'd miss the cold of the mountains,' he grumbles. 'At least there you could keep your beard from catching fire!'

With a deep breath and a muttered prayer to the spirits, Jimli sets off into the heart of the desert, hoping the sun doesn't turn his journey into a roasted dwarf buffet."
