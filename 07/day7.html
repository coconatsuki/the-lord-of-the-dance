The Mountains of Melody

The Climb

After the grueling heat of the Desert of Rhythms, Jimli finally reaches the base of the towering *Mountains of Melody.* As the desert sands fade behind him, the air changes —cooler, lighter, and filled with a faint, melodic hum. Every step he takes seems to stir a note from the rocks, like the mountain itself is singing to him.

“The *Mountains of Melody,* eh?” Jimli mutters, glancing upward. The peaks seem to stretch forever into the sky, their snow-capped summits glowing in the distance. “I've climbed worse, but none that try to hum me off their slopes.”

The path ahead is steep, winding through jagged rocks and narrow ledges. “Alright, mountain,” says Jimli, gripping his axe tightly, “you can hum all you want, but I'm dancing to my own tune.” With that, he begins his ascent.
