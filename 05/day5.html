As the sun dips lower in the sky, casting long shadows across the dunes, Jimli stumbles upon a strange sight: a lizard, about the size of his boot, perched on a rock and moving in a peculiar rhythm.

Jimli raises an eyebrow. "Dancing with a lizard? That’s a new one. If my kin could see me now, they’d have me mining rocks just to get the silliness out!"

The lizard continues its odd dance, twisting and twirling with surprising agility. Jimli watches, fascinated, as the creature seems to be challenging him to a duel of dance moves. "Alright, you scaly show-off," Jimli says, cracking his knuckles. "Let’s see if you’ve got what it takes to keep up with a dwarf!"
