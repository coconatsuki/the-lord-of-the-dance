The Enchanted Forest

The Path of Leaves

The deeper Jimli goes, the more twisted and confusing the forest paths become. "By Thorin’s toenails," he swears, scratching his head. But then he notices the leaves—each one has a unique pattern. "I may not know an oak from an ash, but by Thorin's toenails, I can match patterns faster than any elf!" Jimli mutters, determined to find the correct path as he starts matching the leaves.
