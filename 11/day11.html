The Valley of Steps

Racing through distractions

Jimli's heart thumps louder than a dwarven war drum as he barrels down the winding path. Each step is a reminder of the endless mountains he's scaled, the biting cold he's survived, and that blasted elf at the gate who nearly ruined it all. But now, the real race begins! The elven 'Mix and Match' contest —the one Jimli's dreamed of winning since he could dance on two sturdy feet— is just within his grasp.

If he doesn't make it in time, all those days of hardship will have been for nothing. His breath comes in ragged gasps, sweat dripping down his brow, but there's no turning back now. “Keep it together, Jimli,” he mutters to himself. The festival awaits, and no elf, bird, or twig is going to stop him now. The path stretches on, the ticking clock in his mind growing louder, but his stubborn dwarven spirit refuses to falter. This is his moment. And by the beard of his ancestors, he won't be distracted —not now!
